# Quickstart

Two minutes either way. You sign in once; your OrangeCheck identity is the
account.

## Sign in

Go to [cosign.ochk.io](https://cosign.ochk.io) and choose **sign in**. Start in
seconds with Google, GitHub, or an email one-time code — no password, nothing to
create. Already hold a Bitcoin wallet? Sign in with that for full self-custody.
It’s the same identity across every `.ochk.io` product.

## If you’re building

1. **Set up your profile** — your name and a one-line headline. Add a Lightning
   address so backing and zaps can settle straight to you.
2. **Post a project** at [`/new`](https://cosign.ochk.io/new) — title, the
   one-line pitch, and crucially **what you need** (a co-founder, capital, or
   both). Fill in team, traction, the ask, and your terms in plain English.
3. **Share it.** The first cosigns come from your own audience — post the link.
4. **Respond to cosigns** from your dashboard. Accept a join or a back to open a
   private thread; decline the ones that aren’t a fit.
5. **Post updates** as you make progress — everyone behind you gets notified.

## If you’re backing

1. **Browse** [`/projects`](https://cosign.ochk.io/projects) — search, filter by
   what a founder needs (co-founder / capital / both), and sort by recent, most
   backed, or most behind.
2. **Get behind one:**
    - **join** if you want to build it with them,
    - **back** it as a patron — pay the founder’s Lightning address to settle
      the sats peer-to-peer, or
    - **zap** a little conviction.
3. **Match and talk.** If you joined or backed, the founder accepts and a
   private thread opens. Front-load the real questions.

## What you never have to do

No KYC. No deposit into OrangeCheck. No equity paperwork on the platform. Sats
move wallet-to-wallet; OC holds nothing. See [Trust & safety](/cosign/trust).
