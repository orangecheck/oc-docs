# Quickstart

Six verbs, six quickstarts. Pick the one that matches what you're trying to ship
and jump straight to it — each protocol's quickstart is self-contained, runs on
its own packages, and gets you to a verified artifact in five minutes.

> **Not sure which one?** Read
> [Which protocol do I need?](/getting-started/which-protocol) for a one-page
> decision tree, or [Ecosystem — shared concepts](/ecosystem) for the plumbing
> every protocol shares (canonical message, BIP-322, Nostr kind-30078).

---

## OC Attest — _am_

**Sybil resistance via proof of Bitcoin stake.** Reach for this when you need to
gate something — a route, an airdrop, a Nostr relay, a forum signup — on a
credible economic cost, without running KYC.

- → [**Speedrun: gate an Express route**](/attest/guides/express) — drop-in
  middleware, ten lines.
- [Sign in with Bitcoin](/attest/guides/sign-in-with-bitcoin) ·
  [Filter an airdrop](/attest/guides/airdrop) ·
  [Sybil-filter a Nostr relay](/attest/guides/nostr-relay)
- [OC Attest section overview](/attest) · [HTTP API](/attest/api)

---

## OC Lock — _whisper_

**End-to-end encryption addressed to a Bitcoin address.** Reach for this when
you need to send a sealed payload to whoever currently controls a specific BTC
address — no centralized key server, no account, no broker.

- → [**Lock quickstart**](/lock/quickstart) — register a device, seal an
  envelope, unseal it on the other side.
- [Lock section overview](/lock)

---

## OC Stamp — _declare_

**BIP-322 authorship + Bitcoin-block-anchored priority.** Reach for this when
you need to prove that _you_ said _this_ _no later than_ a specific Bitcoin
block. Replaces PGP + OpenTimestamps + C2PA in one self-contained envelope.

- → [**Stamp quickstart**](/stamp/quickstart) — sign and verify a stamp in 60
  seconds, hosted client or SDK.
- [Stamp section overview](/stamp)

---

## OC Vote — _decide_

**Stake-weighted, sybil-resistant polls.** Reach for this when you need a
deterministic, cross-impl-testable tally where weight comes from Bitcoin (one
address, sats, or sats × days), not from a captured registry.

- → [**Vote quickstart**](/vote/quickstart) — create a poll, cast a ballot,
  verify the tally.
- [Vote section overview](/vote)

---

## OC Agent — _delegate_

**Scoped, revocable signing authority bound to a Bitcoin address.** Reach for
this when you need to delegate signing to a script, bot, or pipeline within a
narrow scope — without handing it your principal key.

- → [**Agent quickstart**](/agent/quickstart) — mint a delegation, sign an
  action, verify the chain back to the BIP-322 root.
- [Agent section overview](/agent)

---

## OC Pledge — _swear_

**Bonded forward-looking commitments.** Reach for this when you need to swear
something is true _now_ about a _future_ outcome, with stake on the line and
public exposure as the enforcement mechanism (never custody, never slashing).

- → [**Pledge quickstart**](/pledge/quickstart) — install the SDK, construct a
  pledge, sign with a wallet, publish.
- [Pledge section overview](/pledge)

---

## What every quickstart shares

Every protocol in the family is built on the same primitive: a BIP-322 signature
from a Bitcoin address over a canonical, human-readable message. Learn that one
shape and the rest of the family pattern-matches.
